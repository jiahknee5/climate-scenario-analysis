Methodology & Business Capability Analysis

CL

CLIMADA Framework

Probabilistic Climate Impact Assessment

Core Methodology

Physics-based probabilistic modeling using peer-reviewed algorithms from ETH Zurich. Integrates meteorological data with economic exposure models to quantify physical climate risks.

Banking Applications

  • Mortgage Portfolio Risk: Property-level hurricane damage probability with uncertainty bounds
  • Corporate Lending: Supply chain disruption analysis for borrowers
  • Insurance Partnerships: Catastrophe bond pricing and reinsurance strategy
  • Capital Planning: Regulatory stress test scenarios (Fed, ECB, BoE)

Competitive Advantages

  • Regulatory acceptance for TCFD and stress testing
  • Peer-reviewed methodology reduces model risk
  • Global coverage for multinational portfolios
  • Integration with economic damage functions

Investment Requirements

High initial cost: $150K-500K implementation. Requires PhD-level risk modeling team. 6-12 month deployment timeline.

ROI Timeline: 18-24 months

CO

Climate-OS Framework

Statistical Trend Analysis & Historical Patterns

Core Methodology

Statistical trend analysis using 40+ years of NOAA hurricane data. Employs linear regression, return period calculations, and confidence interval analysis to identify patterns and project future activity.

Banking Applications

  • Portfolio Screening: Rapid assessment of geographic risk concentration
  • Economic Capital: Historical loss frequency for regulatory capital models
  • Strategic Planning: Long-term trend analysis for business planning
  • Due Diligence: Quick risk assessment for M&A transactions

Competitive Advantages

  • Immediate deployment with existing data teams
  • Low computational requirements
  • Transparent methodology for audit purposes
  • Cost-effective for regional banks

Investment Requirements

Low initial cost: $25K-75K implementation. Can be managed by existing analytics team. 2-4 week deployment timeline.

ROI Timeline: 3-6 months
View Technical Demo
CB

C-bottle Framework

Synthetic Scenario Generation & Rapid Prototyping

Core Methodology

Monte Carlo simulation with synthetic hurricane generation calibrated to historical patterns. Enables rapid scenario testing and what-if analysis using machine learning-enhanced storm modeling.

Banking Applications

  • Scenario Analysis: Custom climate scenarios for strategic planning
  • Product Development: Rapid testing of new insurance products
  • Client Presentations: Interactive risk visualization for wealth management
  • Innovation Labs: Experimental modeling for fintech partnerships

Competitive Advantages

  • Unlimited scenario generation capability
  • Real-time analysis for client meetings
  • Modern visualization for executive presentations
  • Flexible parameter adjustment

Investment Requirements

Medium initial cost: $75K-200K implementation. Requires data science team with ML experience. 3-6 month deployment timeline.

ROI Timeline: 6-12 months
